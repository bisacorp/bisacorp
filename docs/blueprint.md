# **App Name**: BISA Corp Digital

## Core Features:

- Service Showcase: A clear, well-structured overview of BISA Corp's web development, app creation, AI model development, and design solutions.
- Project Portfolio: An organized section displaying past projects with descriptions and imagery, allowing visitors to explore BISA Corp's work.
- About Us Page: A dedicated page providing company information, mission statement, and team details to build trust and credibility.
- Integrated Contact Form: A functional contact form allowing visitors to easily submit inquiries directly from the website.
- AI Services Explainer Tool: An interactive tool that generates example use cases or taglines for AI solutions based on user input, demonstrating BISA Corp's AI capabilities.
- Responsive Design: Ensure the corporate website adapts seamlessly and functions effectively across all device sizes and screen resolutions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Anchor: 'Corporate Professionalism'. Primary color: A deep, authoritative blue (#172B70) to convey stability and trust in a professional context.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, subtle blue-gray (#ECF0F7) to maintain a clean, minimalist aesthetic while complementing the primary blue.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear sky-blue (#2FBBDC) used sparingly for calls to action and highlighting key information, providing a fresh contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, suitable for a professional corporate site.
- Use minimalist, crisp line icons that align with a modern, professional, and uncluttered visual style.
- Adopt a clean, grid-based layout with generous white space to enhance readability and maintain a minimalist aesthetic.
- Subtle and smooth transition animations for page navigation and hover effects on interactive elements, ensuring a polished user experience.
